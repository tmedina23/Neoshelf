Importing will restore your book list and page positions. Books that already exist in your library keep whichever progress is further ahead.

When connected, your books and reading progress are stored in your Google account's hidden app-data space — it never appears in your regular Drive file list and can't be browsed or opened from Drive itself, only from this app. While connected, Drive is the primary copy of your library; this device's local copy is kept as an offline cache.
